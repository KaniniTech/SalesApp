Sales Note (App)

Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 26, 2025

Welcome to Sales Note! We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information, or more accurately, how we don't. Our goal is to be transparent and provide you with a utility that you can trust.

Information We Collect

When you use the App, we collect the following information:

  • Sales Data: Item names, prices, quantities, and any optional notes you record for reference (e.g., “these sales was sold at discount price and customer paid half”).
  • Payment Method Reference: The type of payment method you select (cash, bank, or other). This is stored only as a note for your own reference and is not connected to any financial account or transaction.
  • Business Profile: business Name, Business Address.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use it for any purpose. The app's functionality is entirely self-contained on your device.

Business Name, is used for branding and it is displayed in the Home Screen of the app,its optional and you can delete it anytime by going to the settings Screen

All sales data is stored locally on your device only. The App does not send, transmit, or back up this information to any remote server or cloud service. As developers, we do not have access to your sales records.

Data Retention & Deletion

You can edit or delete any sales data within the app at any time. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data from your device.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services for analytics, advertising, or any other purpose. All functionality is built-in, and your data never leaves your device through our app.

Sales Note does not use or integrate any third-party services, including:

  • Google Analytics or Firebase
  • Advertising or marketing platforms (e.g., AdMob)
  • Cloud storage or syncing systems

While the app itself does not collect or transmit user data, please note that Google Play may collect basic information about app usage, device type, and crash reports. This information is handled by Google under their own Privacy Policy.

Data Security

The security of your data is paramount. Since our application does not collect or store your data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our service. Any data the app works with remains locally on your device and is subject to your device's security measures.

Please note that if your device is lost or the data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.

App Permissions

The App does not require access to sensitive permissions such as camera, contacts, or location. All features work without requesting such permissions.

Children's Privacy

Our application is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can verify and address the issue, although this is highly unlikely given our app's design.
